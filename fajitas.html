Fajitas

Description

An easy but tasty fajita recipe for the whole family to enjoy.

This recipe serves 4

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts, finely sliced
  • 1 red onion, finely sliced
  • 1 red pepper, sliced
  • 1 heaped tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • pinch of ground cumin
  • 2 medium garlic cloves, crushed
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 4-5 drops Tabasco
  • 8 medium tortillas
  • cherry tomatoes, halved
  • cucumber, diced
  • fresh salsa
  • sour cream
  • avocado, sliced
  • chedder cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Mix 1 heaped tbsp smoked paprika, 1 tbsp ground coriander, a pinch of ground cumin, 2 crushed garlic cloves, 4 tbsp olive oil, the juice of 1 lime and 4-5 drops Tabasco together in a bowl with a big pinch each of salt and pepper.
  2. Stir 2 finely sliced chicken breasts, 1 finely sliced red onion and 1 sliced red pepper into the marinade.
  3. Heat a griddle pan until smoking hot and add the chicken and marinade to the pan.
  4. Keep everything moving over a high heat for about 5 mins using tongs until you get a nice charred effect. If your griddle pan is small you may need to do this in two batches.

  6. Put the tortillas in the oven to heat up and serve with the cooked chicken, tomatoes, cucumber, salsa, sour cream, avocado and cheese.
