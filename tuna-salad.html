Tuna Salad

Description

This is delicious, healthy and simple tuna salad recipe for those who are trying to eat well with very little time to cook.

This recipe serves 4

Ingredients

  • 350g baby potatoes
  • French green beans, trimmed
  • pack cherry tomatoes
  • half a red onion, sliced
  • 3 cans of tuna
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • spinach
  • lettuce
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • tbsp of salted butter

Steps

  1. Place baby potatoes in a large pot and cover well with water. Boil for 10 minutes or until you are able to stick a fork through potatoes. Using a slotted spoon, remove potatoes from boiling water and transfer to a plate
  2. Hlave the potatoes and then put two tbsps of butter on top of the potatoes with the minced garlic, so that the butter melts and covers all of the potatoes
  3. Cut up all of the salad ingredients and add to a bowl.
  4. In another bowl, mix together the tuna with the mayyonaise.
  5. Add the tuna to the salad bowl, along with the potatoes and enjoy!
